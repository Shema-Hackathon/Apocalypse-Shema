Revelation 1:12-20

The 7 Golden Lampstands

Theological Meaning

The seven golden lampstands represent the seven churches of Asia Minor and symbolize the presence of Jesus Christ among His people. Each lampstand emits its own light, but this light comes from a single source - Christ Himself.

Historical and Biblical Context

This vision follows the tradition of light symbols in the Old Testament, particularly the seven-branched lampstand of the temple. The number seven symbolizes perfection and fullness, indicating that Christ is present throughout His Church.

Personal Application

This vision reminds us that Christ walks among His Church today. He knows our situations, our struggles, and our victories. As believers, we are called to be light-bearers in a darkened world.
